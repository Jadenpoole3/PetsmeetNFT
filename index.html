How It Began?

 It all started one day on planet Noman. Natural resources were running out and the Pets needed a new place to go. They all packed up in their spaceships and voyage into space in hopes of finding a new home. They crashed into a planet called Earth.

Who Are We?

We’re a community of exciting, spontaneous, and emerging creators ready to meet all new pets around the globe. On the Solana Blockchain, not only will there be new places to discover but all types of pets to meet.

Our Future

This will not be our first release, we have many more things to come. We will not only stamp our name in the NFT space, but create a brand with longevity and impact. As we grow as a community you will have the chance to be apart of our beginning and our future.

Rarities

Each 100% unique and hand-drawn pet is programmatically and randomly generated from over 120 different traits and abilities. Each pet has its own set of unique features that emerge from the following trait options:

  • Skin
  • Background
  • Eyes & Eyewear
  • Clothing
  • Headwear
  • Accessories
  • Tails

Roadmap

Community Giveaways

More than 100 Pets will be given away to early community members, as well as those who participate in contests on Twitter, Discord, and for creating art with their Pets.

Partnerships

We will partner with influencers to raise awareness and engagement for our brand. Members will have access to exclusive giveaways and drops from collaborations, other collections, and contests.

Community Wallet

We will fund the community wallet with 20 Sol. The community will decide what the funds will be used for.

Donations

We will be donating a portion of all our sales to animal shelters. We will also use this time to start exploring new ideas for season 2.

Merch

We will release exclusive merch to Pet Parents and also release merch to the public. We will work with bigger brands to customize your Pet in the coolest gear. 

Metaverse

We will begin developing our virtual reality game where you will be able to take care of your pet in the Metaverse. You will also be able to meet other pets and explore new places all over the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will the pet cost?

Each nft will be 0.08 solana + gas fees .

Where and how can I buy a pet?

We will be minting on our website. You can acquire a Pet by connecting your wallet (such as Trust, Phantom) to our website and minting your own Pet upon launch. Remember, resources are limited! Yes. You’re able to mint up to 20 Pets per transaction.

What are our plans?

We will be donating a portion from each sale to animal charites. We will be the next blue-chip NFT with not only bringing a new virtual reality gaming experience, but also turning your real life pets into NFT’s. Look at our Roadmap for more.
